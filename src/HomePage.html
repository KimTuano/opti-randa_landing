    WHERE DOES CLIQ AND DINE CAME FROM?

    System Plus College Foundation

    A group thesis maker from SPCF Miranda envisioned creating such a system to streamline the administrative tasks in a hotel and to make it easier for guests to order food, aligning with the latest technologies in the world.

    We will provide you with satisfaction that you won't find elsewhere. Welcome to Cliq and Dine!

    OUR OBJECTIVES

    Mission

    We strive to seamlessly integrate Cliq and Dine with the daily lives of both guests and administrators, creating a cohesive ecosystem that fosters efficiency, simplicity, and reliability in the food ordering process.



    Vision

    To redefine the dining experience by being the premier food ordering system that seamlessly connects guests with a diverse range of culinary delights, while empowering administrators with an intuitive and efficient platform.
